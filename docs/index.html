Let's make Chai Sugar Cookies !! 🍪



Today we are going to attempt to make our version of Taylor Swift's chai sugar cookies.



Ingredients and Measurements 🥚 🧈

For the cookies:
  • 1 twinnings chai tea bag
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup or 1 stick of unsalted butter at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
For the glaze:
  • 1 1/2 cups of powdered sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • 3 tablespoons of whole milk

Steps 📝

(No mixer needed)
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Line the baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Using a fork or whisk beat the butter in a large bowl. Then add the vegetable oil, granulated sugar, powdered sugar, and the chai tea bag spices and fully incorporate it with the butter.
  4. Then add the egg and vanilla extract and mix in with the previous mixture. Once that is done stir in the flour, baking soda, and salt all at once into that same mixture.
  5. At this point we should now have a soft dough. Let it refrigerate for an hour or freeze for 15 minutes.
  6. Once time passes take out the dough from the fridge and use a tablespoon to scoop out the desired cookie dough amount. For example, use 2 tablespoons for a bigger cookie and 1 tablespoon for smaller cookies. Then place about 2 inches apart in the baking sheet with the parchment paper.
  7. When the oven is ready bake the cookies for 13 minutes.
  8. While the cookies are baking grab a medium bowl and whisk together the glaze ingredients.
  9. Once the cookies are done pour about a teaspoon of glaze or the desired amount.
